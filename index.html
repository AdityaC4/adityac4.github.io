Research Interests. I am interested in questions about correctness -- about whether the systems people rely on every day actually do what they are supposed to do. Software is full of compromises that made sense at the time and then quietly became the norm. I find those gaps interesting. My current interests are in correctness at the intersection of formal methods, ML/NN, and compilers.
